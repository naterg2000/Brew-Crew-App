Backend

User: Anyone using the BrewCrew app
- first name, last name, profile picture, birthday

Company: an organization of employees
- name, corporate phone number

Employee: a team member, barista, lead, or manager
- extends user
- wage, avaliability
- can request days off
- can change avaliability

Manager
- phone number for employees

Lead
- phone number for employees (optional visibility)

Barista/ Team member
- have avaliabilities

Schedule: a display of shifts for each employee
- employees can see who else will be working with them that shift
